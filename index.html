Xplorer - Taiwan's First 3D AI Home Buying Experience

Xplorer integrates Real Estate-specific Large Language Models (LLMs) with 3D scene reconstruction technology.

I Built a 3D reconstruction team of 4 from scratch. Led the development of 3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS) based recon- struction, successfully introducing it into production services.

  • Improved indoor real estate property reconstruction quality by fine-tuning hyperparameters and introducing mesh ini- tialization to 3DGS, resulting in 90% customer satisfaction ratings.
  • Collaborated with iOS backend, DevOps and QA teams to successfully deploy 3D reconstruction to production, enabling visualization of 100+ properties per month.
  • Optimized computational cost and reconstruction quality balance, reducing cloud computing expenses by 40% while maintaining visual quality with PSNR greater than 23.
  • Enhanced SfM results for downstream tasks (3DGS reconstruction and roomplan texturing) by integrating Deep Learn- ing (DL) based feature extraction/matching and balancing pose prior and reprojection error in bundle adjustment.
  • Improved SfM pipeline efficiency 5 times by implementing multi batch-size feature matching and GPU linear solver.
